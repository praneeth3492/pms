Home to Engaging Classes and Transformative Learning Experiences

At Manchester Global School, we are committed to providing students an extraordinary educational experience that will set them up for success in life. We will cater to students from Nursery to Grade 12, providing them with a strong foundation for their educational journey.

Our prestigious International Integrated Curriculum and National Curriculum perfectly integrate, ensuring a seamless transition as your child progresses through each academic year.

Our dedicated team of educators will create a supportive and engaging environment that will nurture every aspect of your child's growth from the very beginning. We understand that education is not just about textbooks and exams, but also about promoting cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development. Through hands-on activities, interactive lessons, and a range of subjects, we will ignite the spark of curiosity within each child, encouraging them to explore, question, and discover the world around them. However, at Manchester Global School, education is much more than just academics. We will work to instil in your child the values and fundamental life skills that will support them throughout every stage of life. Children will gain knowledge of the value of resilience, empathy, and collaboration in our energetic classrooms. They will have insightful discussions, impart their distinctive viewpoints, and celebrate their individuality. By establishing a secure environment where they can freely express themselves, we hope to give children a sense of community and self-worth.

We understand that as parents, you want the best for your child. That is why we have carefully crafted a thorough curriculum that goes beyond traditional subjects. Your child will delve into the arts, explore the wonders of science, uncover the mysteries of mathematics, and embark on exciting literary journeys. Our goal is to inspire a passion for learning, allowing your child's true potential to flourish.
