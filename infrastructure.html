CAMPUS

Manchester Global School is a haven of exceptional infrastructure designed to nurture your child's educational journey. Our campus, which spans nearly 20 acres in Hyderabad, offers a peaceful fusion of modernism and tranquillity. The campus is designed to offer a totally immersive educational experience with a proposed built-up area of about 6 lakh square feet.



Our classrooms have been meticulously constructed to promote a motivating and immersive learning environment. Our laboratories serve as cutting-edge centres that spark a desire for inquiry, invention, and problem-solving via the execution of Chemistry and Biology experiments, as well as explorations into the fields of Physics and Robotics.

Our laboratories serve as cutting-edge centres of scientific inquiry outside of the classroom. Subject-based Advanced Labs give students practical experience in a variety of fields, allowing them to better understand concepts and hone crucial scientific abilities. Our laboratories spark a desire for inquiry, invention, and problem-solving via the execution of Chemistry and Biology experiments, as well as explorations into the fields of Physics and Robotics.

To ensure the well-being and comfort of our students, Manchester Global School offers state-of-the-art boarding facilities that meet global standards. Our separate hostel buildings for boys and girls can accommodate 600 students, providing a nurturing environment for academic, social, and emotional growth. Our fully-furnished staff building offers more than 50 flats, ensuring our dedicated faculty from around the world feel right at home within our close-knit community.  



At the heart of our campus, you will discover our awe-inspiring convention centre, a magnificent structure spanning over 70,000 square feet. The first floor features a spacious dining area for 800 children. On the second floor, our expansive library awaits. Spanning 8,000 square feet and accommodating 250 people, it is one of the largest in India and the largest among international schools in South India. Moving up, the third and fourth floors house an auditorium for 700 people. Finally, on the fifth floor, a breathtaking surprise awaits - our Sky Observatory and Mini Planetarium. With telescopes and celestial wonders, it will be the first-of-its-kind in South India for an international school.



On the third and fourth floors, our state-of-the-art auditorium awaits, ready to host captivating performances, presentations, and events. With a mezzanine level, it can accommodate up to 700 people, providing a grand setting for showcasing talents and fostering a love for the arts.

Take a step further to the fifth floor, where a breathtaking surprise awaits - our Sky Observatory and Mini Planetarium. With telescopes and celestial wonders, it will be the first-of-its-kind in South India for an international school. Here, your child will embark on astronomical adventures, expanding their horizons beyond the ordinary.



As you explore our campus, you will encounter our open-air amphitheatre, a vibrant space that can accommodate upwards of 150 people. It serves as a hub for cultural events, dramatic performances, and community gatherings.



Beyond these key features, Manchester Global School boasts trend-setting infrastructure tailored to empower your child's learning journey. From state-of-the-art sports fields for football, rugby, cricket, swimming, and tennis to arts and design studios, robotics, and gaming and virtual reality (VR) studios, our facilities inspire innovation, exploration, and holistic development.
